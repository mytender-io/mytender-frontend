Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about mytender.io? We’ve compiled answers to the most common inquiries to help you get the most out of our platform. Explore the FAQ below to find quick solutions and helpful insights.

How is my data stored securely?

Data security might not be thrilling, but neither is leaving the front door unlocked—let’s ensure your information stays safe and sound. Any client data is stored in an isolated virtual private environment, meaning there is no potential data contamination or the risk of data being leaked in a public environment. In fact, it is very important that businesses do not use public AI models such as ChatGPT when using confidential data, as this data can be used to train public models and risks being leaked into the public domain. Within mytender.io’s platform data is secured at rest and in transit with SSO authentication and Amazon's EC2 Instance, and all stored within the UK.

How does the AI produce bespoke and relevant answers?

The system utilises Retrieval-Augmented-Generation (RAG) meaning it analyses your company information and previous bid material, whilst considering the tender specification and implicit win themes presented by the tender. With this information we are able to use AI to synthesise a high-scoring, bespoke first draft, whilst saving you lots of time (yay). As well as base RAG the platform uses many clever databases and AI methodologies to ensure all output is to the best quality possible. If you have an interest in AI systems and want to learn more, feel free to reach out to Jamie our CTO, he loves AI.

What happens if my competitors are also using mytender.io, will we have similar responses?

If you have semi-intelligent competitors, then this is an important question for you, as they are probably using mytender.io already to write their bids for them. So, does this mean your bids will be outputted similarly? No, our platform repurposes your company information, notes, tone of voice and edits into your bid- so your responses may look completely different to your competitors that’s already making headway by using mytender.io for their bidding.

Can I be marked down by tender assessors for using ai in my bids?

As of 25/03/2024, The Cabinet’s Office posted a public procurement notice (PPN) stating that bidders will not be marked down for the use of AI within bids. With the government now using AI to help create tenders, it would be quite unfair for them to stop you using AI in the responses. Your biggest concern should actually be- if you’re not using AI in your bidding you will actually be at a disadvantage against other bidders who have more time and resources to submit better bids.
